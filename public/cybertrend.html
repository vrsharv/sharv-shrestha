As more companies migrate to the cloud, ensuring the security of cloud-based systems and protecting sensitive data is crucial in 2024.

Organizations are focusing on building resilience to withstand and recover from cyberattacks, emphasizing incident response and backup strategies.

Cyber Inequity

Quantum Security

The emergence of quantum computing poses future challenges, prompting organizations to explore quantum-safe cryptography.

Supply Chain Security

The security of global supply chains is a major concern, with businesses focusing on protecting sensitive data shared across networks.

Regulations and Compliance

New regulations around data privacy and security will require businesses to adopt stricter cybersecurity standards.
